What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites to remember information about your visit and improve your experience.

What We Store

TypeWhatPurposeDuration

Important: Photo Privacy

Photos you upload in the frame creator are processed entirely in your browser memory. They are never stored in cookies, localStorage, or any server. They disappear when you close the editor.

Third-Party Cookies

Amazon.in sets cookies when you click affiliate product links. These are governed by Amazon's Cookie Policy. We have no control over these cookies.

Managing localStorage

You can clear all locally stored ZetsyBuy Gift data by going to your browser Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cookies and site data. This will reset admin data and frame settings.
