Privacy Policy

✅ Last updated: December 2024

ZetsyBuy Gift ("we","our","us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Photos & Images

Photos you upload are processed entirely in your browser (device). We never upload, store, or transmit your photos to any server. Your images never leave your device.

2. Data We Collect

We collect anonymous usage data (pages visited, browser type) to improve the service. We use localStorage to save your preferences (frame settings, templates) locally on your device only.

3. Amazon Affiliate Program

ZetsyBuy Gift participates in the Amazon Associates Program. When you click product links and make purchases on Amazon, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. Amazon may set their own cookies — refer to Amazon's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Cookies & localStorage

We use browser localStorage (not server cookies) to save preferences like your last frame template. No personal data is stored on our servers. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use Google Analytics (anonymous usage stats). We do not sell your data to any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You can clear all locally stored data anytime by clearing your browser's localStorage/cookies. There is no account or server-side data to delete.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email: hello@zetsybuy.in
